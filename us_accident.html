US Accident Prediction


Analytic Objective
  • Acident factors
  • Acident rates by state in a day
  • Accident rates by road
  • Use the data to prevent and avoid an upcoming accident
Data Description

Dataset about car accident, which covers 49 states of the USA from 2016-2021. Currently, there are about 2.8 million accident records in this dataset and contain 47 attributes.



Data visualization

Model Implementation
  • Model : Logistic Regression
  • Target : Accident severity at level 4
  • Interesting Variables : Weather Condition

Discussion and Conclusion
  • State with the most car accidents is Califonia.
  • Street with the most car accidents is I-95 N.
  • City with the most average car accidents in a day is Miami, FLorida.
  • The weather does not significantly affect car accidents.
